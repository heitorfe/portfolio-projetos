Data Science Projects

Sales Prediction

I used Python to build a Machine Learning model to predict the sales of each store, of the 3 thousand registered, in the next 6 weeks. The model was put into production and can be requested via API by Telegram, just having access to the internet. The model had a prediction of 90% of the real value, allowing the CFO to make decisions based on the future revenue of each unit of the store and thus, be able to make investments without losses.
